Jongware's scripts

Jongware (the real name Theunis de Jong) was one of the best scripters in the world. He passed away on November 28, 2020, at the age of 54. His site — http://www.jongware.com — is out of service now so the scripts he shared there are unavailable anymore. Here I am going to post his scripts. If you have his scripts and other materials — e. g. the descriptions of his scripts, screenshots taken from his site, etc. — and would like to have them posted on my site, please send them to me. I want to restore his heritage and make it available to the general public.
