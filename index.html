Welcome to my website!

Dear visitor,

I created this site to share my personal experience with scripting Adobe applications, mainly InDesign.

I've been working in InDesign since 2004 and automated all kinds of things we used to do manually. Besides InDesign, I also write scripts for InCopy, Photoshop, Bridge. I write mostly in JavaScript, but know AppleScript and Visual Basic as well.

In producing this site, I have tried to “give back” something to the World Wide Web, which has given me so much. What do I have to offer?

  • I made a page with links to interesting scripts sorted by categories. My idea is to aggregate a big collection of scripts, scattered all over the web, in one place.
  • For those who are interested in developing their own scripts, I post the scripts I made for my workflow, useful snippets of code, and miscellaneous scripting tips & tricks sorted by alphabet. I have been monitoring Adobe InDesign scripting forum for 22 years and I have collected a great deal of interesting information I want to share on this site.
  • Check out my new crowd-funding projects idea and my first crowd-funded script — called Find Text in Location — sponsored by and made in cooperation with The Treasures of GREP group on Facebook.
