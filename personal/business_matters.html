Business matters

Usually I charge by hours — $15 per hour — since clients usually ask for new features, change specifications, etc. during development.
If you wish, you can fix a maximum budget for your project so that I wouldn't charge you more than you expect.
On average, one script costs $50—150. The most expensive (and one of the most complex) I ever made — $1500 — it took a few weeks to write.

Payment method is one of the following:

  • PayPal — to my e-mail: askoldich [at] yahoo [dot] com
  • Western Union
  • MoneyGram
  • SWIFT
  • RIA

I never share my clients’ materials: documents, fonts, images, etc. I reserve the right to post my scripts, or parts of their code, on my site and make screenshots from my clients’ documents and use them for the descriptions of the above-mentioned scripts.
If a client doesn’t want me to publish the scripts written for him/her and/or the screenshots taken from his/her files, he/she should inform me about this beforehand.
